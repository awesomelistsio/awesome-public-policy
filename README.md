## Foundations & Concepts

- [Public Policy Overview](https://www.britannica.com/topic/public-policy) – High-level explanation of public policy and its role in governance.
- [Policy Cycle](https://www.unodc.org/e4j/en/anti-corruption/module-3/key-issues/the-policy-cycle.html) – Framework for policy formulation, implementation, and evaluation.
- [Evidence-Based Policy](https://www.oecd.org/gov/evidence-based-policy-making.htm) – Using empirical evidence to inform policy decisions.
- [Cost-Benefit Analysis](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/c/cost-benefitanalysis.asp) – Analytical method for evaluating policy trade-offs.
- [Regulatory Impact Analysis](https://www.oecd.org/gov/regulatory-policy/ria.htm) – Assessing economic and social impacts of regulation.

## Policy Research & Think Tanks

- [Brookings Institution](https://www.brookings.edu/) – Public policy research across economics, governance, and social issues.
- [RAND Corporation](https://www.rand.org/) – Policy analysis and research for public and private sectors.
- [Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS)](https://www.csis.org/) – Policy research on global security and international affairs.
- [Peterson Institute for International Economics](https://www.piie.com/) – Research on global economic policy.
- [Chatham House](https://www.chathamhouse.org/) – Independent policy institute focusing on international affairs.

## Government & Multilateral Institutions

- [OECD](https://www.oecd.org/) – International organization focused on economic and social policy.
- [World Bank](https://www.worldbank.org/) – Global development policy and financing institution.
- [International Monetary Fund (IMF)](https://www.imf.org/) – Global monetary cooperation and financial stability organization.
- [United Nations](https://www.un.org/) – Multilateral institution addressing global policy challenges.
- [European Commission](https://commission.europa.eu/) – Executive body shaping EU public policy.

## Policy Data & Statistics

- [World Bank Open Data](https://data.worldbank.org/) – Global development and policy-relevant datasets.
- [OECD Data](https://data.oecd.org/) – Comparable international policy indicators.
- [UN Data](https://data.un.org/) – Global economic, social, and environmental statistics.
- [Eurostat](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat) – European Union statistical office.
- [IPUMS](https://ipums.org/) – Harmonized census and survey microdata for policy research.

## Policy Analysis & Evaluation

- [Policy Analysis Toolkit](https://www.worldbank.org/en/topic/governance/brief/policy-analysis) – Tools for evaluating public policies.
- [Impact Evaluation](https://www.worldbank.org/en/programs/sief-trust-fund/impact-evaluation) – Methods for assessing policy effectiveness.
- [Difference-in-Differences](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/d/difference-in-differences.asp) – Econometric technique for causal policy analysis.
- [Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs)](https://www.povertyactionlab.org/methodology) – Experimental approach to policy evaluation.
- [Cost-Effectiveness Analysis](https://www.cdc.gov/policy/analysis/cea.html) – Comparing policy outcomes relative to costs.

## Economics & Social Policy

- [CORE-Econ](https://www.core-econ.org/) – Open-access economics resources for policy analysis.
- [NBER](https://www.nber.org/) – Economic research informing public policy.
- [Institute for Fiscal Studies (IFS)](https://ifs.org.uk/) – Research on taxation, welfare, and public spending.
- [World Inequality Database](https://wid.world/) – Data on income and wealth inequality.
- [Our World in Data](https://ourworldindata.org/) – Data-driven research on global social issues.

## Technology, Digital & AI Policy

- [OECD AI Policy Observatory](https://oecd.ai/) – Global AI policy analysis and resources.
- [AI Now Institute](https://ainowinstitute.org/) – Research on social implications of AI.
- [GovTech Lab](https://www.hks.harvard.edu/centers/mrcbg/programs/govtech) – Technology innovation in government.
- [Digital Public Goods Alliance](https://digitalpublicgoods.net/) – Promoting open digital infrastructure for public policy.
- [Center for Democracy & Technology](https://cdt.org/) – Policy research on digital rights and technology governance.

## Climate, Environment & Sustainability Policy

- [UNEP](https://www.unep.org/) – United Nations environment policy organization.
- [Climate Policy Initiative](https://www.climatepolicyinitiative.org/) – Analysis of climate finance and policy.
- [IEA](https://www.iea.org/) – Energy policy and sustainability research.
- [Global Carbon Atlas](https://globalcarbonatlas.org/) – Data on global carbon emissions.

## Health & Education Policy

- [World Health Organization (WHO)](https://www.who.int/) – Global health policy and guidance.
- [CDC Policy Analysis](https://www.cdc.gov/policy/index.html) – Public health policy research and tools.
- [UNESCO](https://www.unesco.org/) – Education, science, and cultural policy.
- [OECD Education](https://www.oecd.org/education/) – Comparative education policy analysis.
- [Institute of Education Sciences (IES)](https://ies.ed.gov/) – Education research and evaluation.

- [Administrative Law Overview](https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/administrative_law) – Legal framework for public administration.
- [Transparency International](https://www.transparency.org/) – Anti-corruption policy research.
- [Open Government Partnership](https://www.opengovpartnership.org/) – International initiative for transparent governance.
- [World Justice Project](https://worldjusticeproject.org/) – Rule of law data and policy analysis.
